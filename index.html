The right school.

The right price tag.

Our elementary schools are outdated and in need of costly repairs. They were all built in the 1960s and no longer meet current educational, safety, or accessibility standards.

The new PreK-5 school is the best solution. It's modern, efficient, brings all students and teachers together—and most importantly, it's more cost-effective than repairing or rebuilding three separate schools. This option and its design reflect years of community input and were developed with educators, families, and the community in mind.

Building a much needed PreK-5 school relies on your vote.
